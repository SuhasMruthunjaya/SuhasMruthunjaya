Detailed Description

POWER_SUPPLY Class.

The Power Supply is the main component for any devices to work. It is passed through a Voltage Regulator to regulate the high Voltage output, which is necessary as some devices has a specific desired voltage for them to function. The Class has two functions to Power ON and Power OFF the Supplier.
