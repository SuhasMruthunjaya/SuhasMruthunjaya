Detailed Description

Voltage Regulator Class.

There is a need for Regulating the Voltage recieved from the Power supply unit, as most of the devices works at 5V DC, whereas the output from the relays via the Power Supply unit are usually more than the maximum voltage. The Class Voltage_Regulator takes in the Actual_Voltage as input and step down it to a reasonable value.
